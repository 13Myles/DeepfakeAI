Applications of Deepfake AI

Here are two real-world examples of Deepfake AI in action:

1. Deepfake Video of President Zelensky

In 2022, a deepfake video emerged of Ukrainian President Zelensky telling troops to surrender. The altered footage was quickly debunked but highlighted the risks of misinformation.

2. The Irishman Movie

In the 2019 film "The Irishman," Deepfake AI was used to de-age actors, creating realistic younger versions for flashback scenes.
