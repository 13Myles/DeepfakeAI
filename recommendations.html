Recommendations

To mitigate the risks and harness the benefits of Deepfake AI, the following measures are recommended:

  • Regulations: Establish clear laws and policies to prevent misuse while encouraging innovation.
  • Awareness: Educate the public on identifying and reporting deepfakes.
  • Technology: Develop AI tools to detect deepfake content effectively.
  • Ethics: Foster ethical guidelines among AI developers and researchers.

Implementing these strategies can help society navigate the challenges of Deepfake AI responsibly.
